The Nuggetarian Union

Common Citizen Information

The Nuggetarian Union's government is offering this page as a way to easily access key information that citizens may be looking for.

The current tax rate for both Sales and Income is 5%. The tax rate for Citizens is 7% and Businesses is 10%. The next tax collection date is the currently scheduled election date.

The next election for the Nuggetarian Union is April 10th, 2025. You can vote online or at a booth on election day. Incumbent president Christopher Higgins is the only one running in the election (Committee of himself, Fisher, and Mackenzie).

The presidential comittee consists of President Christopher Higgins (Finance), Officer Michael Hussey-Fisher (Farming Planner), and Officer Mackenzie Moores (Livestock and Food Products).

There are no current threats for sovereignty or risks to individuals that the government must make citizens notified of. This page, as well as a notification of some sort, will go out should this change, however President Higgins signed Series 8 legislation on April 6th, 2025.
