The Nuggetarian Union

Update From President Higgins

April 15th, 2025
President Christopher Higgins

Back in March, I founded the Nuggetarian Union and continued my consecutive rule as president, a lineage that I have held for now 6 consecutive empires. Currently, I am serving the third term of my presidency, after being extablished as the first president by default, and running unopposed the second time.

I had a few goals. Moderation, which has been achieved, progression, which has been achieved, basic rights, which has been achieved, and so much more.

Some of you might have already seen this coming. Last term, I dismissed the government after establishing the office of the Vice President, and now, it is time to set the precedent. I have now set up the empire to be left in good hands to a potential successor.

Last night, I informed the members of my Committee, Vice President and Officer Fisher, and Officer Mackenzie, the same thing that I decided I would do last term, and the same thing I am announcing today.

I intend to resign as the president of the Nuggetarian Union next Tuesday.

If you count all my empires, I've been serving consecutively since the Ultimate Nuggetarian Empire over 3 years ago. It's now time for a pause, at least, from such a high title.

With my resignation, I have asked Vice President/Committee Officer Fisher to become the next President. He has agreed, and I have already drafted the piece of legislation required to sign for both my resignation and his ascension to the presidency.

I will still continue to serve in the government, and I have offered my effort to Fisher to serve on his committee to continue my duty as the officer of Finance.

It has been a privilege to serve as the president of the Nuggetarian Union, which, by large, affected the entire world.

Thank you.
