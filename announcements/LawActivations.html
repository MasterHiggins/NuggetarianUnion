Temporary Law Repeals

April 2nd, 2025
Government of the NU

The president has updated the active status of previously inactive laws.

Act 6-2-3, the Right to Minimum Wage, has been restored.

7-2, Universal Basic Income, has been restored.

7-8, the Safe Connected Empire Act, is still temporarily restricted due to lack of development.

7-10, the Restart Subsidy Act, has been reactivated.

7-15, the Iron farm Regulation Act, has been resigned, meaning Iron Farms are now illegal.

7-18, the Government Salary Act, will remain restricted to account for the new structure.

7-22, the Soft Reset Accord, will remain on hold for another 2 weeks.

7-24, the Deficit Act, has been barred due to lack of Iron to borrow.

A policy from the last empire, which was government supplied armour, is still temporarily restricted for at least 2 more weeks.
