The Nuggetarian Union

Term 3 Budget

April 11th, 2025
President Christopher Higgins

Today, I signed the budget for Term 3, which reflects sustainability and growth for citizens in the Nuggetarian Union.

0.5% of this budget will be going toward the salary of the President at the end of the term.

15% of the budget is to be split across the comittee members' salaries to be paid at the end of the term, capped to 512 Iron each.

0.5% will be paid out to IT for the website and other out of game services.

4% will be towards the military and protecting the new found sovereignty of the Union.

10% of the budget will go towards the continued construction efforts in Nuggetaria, the heart province.

1024 Iron will be kept aside for Universal Basic Income and other payments.

7% of the budget will go towards aid provided within the Foreign Rights Act.

The rest will be saved in the event it is needed or a program is created. The national reserve currently stands at roughly 6000 Iron. The budget will scale with the reserve as it increases.
