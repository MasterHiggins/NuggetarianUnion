The Nuggetarian Union

President Signs Empire Establishing Laws

March 22nd, 2025
Government of the NU

President Christopher Higgins has signed a number of laws to help establish the Nuggetarian Union.

The President signed 9-1 and 9-2, which provides rights as well as government operations, establishing the empire. He also signed 9-3, which forces budgets to be easily available to the public, and reports on where it all went. He signed 9-4, which was foreign policy that can be changed by future administrations. 9-5 and 9-6 provide limits to presidential power as well as how to impeach a president. 9-7E is the President's executive order to set tax rates. 9-8 and 9-9 provide restrictions on how the Government can collect taxes. 9-10 and 9-11 give comittee members protection and the president powers over the comittee. 9-12E the President's budget for this term, and 9-13 is limitations on brewing.

All laws were drafted on March 20th, 2025 and signed on March 22nd, 2025 by President Christopher Higgins.
