Tax Tool

This tool is a way to calculate the total amount you owe in tax.

If you are a individual using this tool, then you will only need the individual and income tax field filled out to easily determine how much you owe. If you are a business, then you will only need the business and sales tax fields filled out. If you are an individual who also owns a business, then all fields need to be filled out to accurately determine what you owe/are owed.

For the sales tax form, please fill out what you owe in sales tax before adding the sales tax rate. for example, if the sales tax is 10% and you made 100 Iron in sales, then enter 100 and not 10. This way, the tool will account for the tax you have already collected.

If you are subject to alternate taxation, then check the box to adjust the rate alternatively.

Please note all taxes are due on election day.






Total Tax: 0

If your total is less than 0, then the government owes you that amount

In the event of a tax discrepancy, you will need the generated reference number.

When using this tool, please exclude anything taxed previously, as the government will not take that into account, and only tax new income. Individual/Business Tax is a rate that taxes anything that came in over the term before the election. Sales Tax is something a business might charge you at the register so they do not have to cover the tax. Income Tax is a tax you may encounter when you get a paycheque. An employer may take this out of your paycheque, but either way, enter the amount you earned rather than the amount you recieved, then check with the payer to confirm if they paid the tax on your behalf.

Please note that this tool is not meant as an absolute way to determine taxes. Agents have the right to write off whcihever amount they feel is fair, and this tool is only meant to have a general estimate. The government will consult the records that it has when trying to determine the new amount to tax. If you lost Iron, then they will not be entering the amount you lost, and may only tax one or two of the fields you entered. Ultimately, this is the same tool they will use, except they have more information and powers to work with. For more tax information for the Nuggetarian Union, click here.
