Presidential Comittee for Term 1

President Christopher Higgins has now selected and appointed the members for the first presidential comittee.

President Christopher Higgins (Finance)

President Christopher Higgins will be serving on the comittee as the finance officer, allowing him to easily manage finances for the nation's budget this term.

Officer Michael Hussey-Fisher (Farming Planner)

Officer Michael Hussey-Fisher will be serving on the comittee as the farming planner, allowing him to easily manage the nation's food supply and plan for the future, whether it be food, fish, or whatever else over the next two weeks.

The President will not act as a member of the comittee in the case of impeachment or "comittee first" approaches that may come before presidential duties.
