Presidential Comittee for Term 3

President Christopher Higgins has now selected and appointed the members for the revised third presidential comittee.

Officer Christopher Higgins (Finance)

Officer Christopher Higgins will be serving on the comittee as the finance officer, allowing him to easily manage finances for the nation's budget this term.

Officer Elissa Chippett (Vice President, President, Northern Affairs)

Officer Elissa Chippett will be serving on the comittee as the Officer of Northern Affairs, allowing her to easily manage the nation's foreign relations over the next two weeks.

Officer Mackenzie Moores (Livestock)

Officer Mackenzie Moores will be serving on the comittee as the Officer of Livestock, allowing her to easily manage the nation's livestock and food items over the next two weeks.
